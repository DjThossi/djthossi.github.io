SmokeTests

Why you should try to burn down your production environment

Talk Abstract

Today it's crystal clear why we need unit tests. Even integration and acceptance tests are quite common but who is making sure that your pages are working in production environment?

I'd like to show in detail how smoke tests will help achieving this goal and why you should try to burn down your production server.

This is a complete new talk I'm preparing right now. I want to show that smoke tests are a good way to approve that all your critical URLs are working. I will show how the workflow of deployments to production environments could look like. How the correct Server setup will help to get SmokeTests running and how we make sure that SmokeTests are not affecting our customers.
